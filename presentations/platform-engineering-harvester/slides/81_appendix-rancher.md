# <span class="accent">Rancher</span> — Kubernetes management<span class="dot">.</span>

<p class="lede" style="margin-top: 20px; max-width: 80ch;">
SUSE's platform for provisioning and operating Kubernetes everywhere — data center, cloud and edge — and the control plane that ties Harvester in.
</p>

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(2, 1fr); gap: 24px; margin-top: 40px;">
  <div class="surface" style="padding: 28px;">
    <div style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: 600;">Cluster lifecycle</div>
    <p style="font-size: 17px; line-height: 1.45; margin-top: 8px; color: var(--fg-muted);">
      Provision, upgrade and manage clusters across providers from one place — including guest clusters on Harvester.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="surface" style="padding: 28px;">
    <div style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: 600;">Central auth &amp; RBAC</div>
    <p style="font-size: 17px; line-height: 1.45; margin-top: 8px; color: var(--fg-muted);">
      One identity, projects and policies enforced consistently across every connected cluster.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="surface" style="padding: 28px;">
    <div style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: 600;">Apps &amp; catalog</div>
    <p style="font-size: 17px; line-height: 1.45; margin-top: 8px; color: var(--fg-muted);">
      Fleet GitOps, Helm catalog and integrated monitoring/logging out of the box.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div class="surface" style="padding: 28px;">
    <div style="font-size: 20px; font-weight: 600;">Harvester driver</div>
    <p style="font-size: 17px; line-height: 1.45; margin-top: 8px; color: var(--fg-muted);">
      Harvester registers as an infrastructure provider — RKE2/K3s nodes land as VMs on Harvester.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
